What's inside the playbook

46 steps. Seven sections. Zero guesswork.

Every screen has a screenshot. Every action has context. Click "Next Step" when you've completed each step.

Section 01 · Ecosystem Signup
Sign up across Pancake, POS & Botcake
  • Sign in to pancake.biz with Facebook
  • Complete the Meta sign-up flow
  • Activate Facebook & Instagram pages
  • Sign up for Pancake POS & Botcake
Section 02 · Livechat
Unified inbox setup
  • Sync messages & comments from Meta Business Suite
  • Set up tags & saved replies (shortcuts like /pr)
  • Add staff to your team inbox
  • Install the Pancake Chrome Extension
  • Create your first order inside a chat
Section 03 · POS Details
Pancake POS for fulfillment
  • Activate shops in Pancake POS
  • Batch-update orders
  • Set currency & shop information
  • Add your first product (basic + advanced info)
Section 04 · Botcake
AI chatbot for your channels
  • Sign up for Botcake
  • Connect Messenger / Instagram / Zalo
  • Build message flows
  • Set up automation (Welcome Message)
Section 05 · Botcake AI
Turn the bot smart
  • Create an AI Agent with prompt + instructions
  • Top up your Pancake Wallet (min $10)
  • Attach Wallet to Botcake
Section 06 · WhatsApp API & CAPI
Connect WABA + send purchase events
  • Meta Embedded Signup flow
  • Create new WhatsApp Business Account (or connect existing)
  • Fill business info — name, category, website
  • Add phone number & verify via OTP
  • Configure Conversion API (CAPI) for purchase signals
Section 07 · CTWA Campaign
Launch your first Click-to-WhatsApp ad
  • Choose your Meta campaign objective
  • Streamlined vs. Manual setup
  • Configure the Ad Set with WhatsApp messaging
  • Route ad messages into Pancake
